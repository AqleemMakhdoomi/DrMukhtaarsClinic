About Us

At Dr. Mukhtar's Imaging Centre, we are dedicated to providing exceptional radiology services with a focus on patient care and comfort. Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with the latest imaging technology, ensuring accurate and timely diagnoses.

Our team of experienced radiologists and technicians are committed to delivering personalized care to each patient. We understand the importance of accurate imaging in the diagnosis and treatment of medical conditions, and we strive to provide the highest quality services.
